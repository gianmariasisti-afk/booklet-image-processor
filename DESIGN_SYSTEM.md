# Booklet Image Processor - Design System

## International Typographic Style Guidelines

### Color Palette
- **Primary Canvas**: Pure white (`#FFFFFF`)
- **Accent**: Bold red (`#E63946` or `#DC2626`)
- **Text & Structure**: Crisp black (`#000000`)
- **Borders & Dividers**: Fine black lines (`#000000`, 1px)
- **Backgrounds**: White with subtle gray for secondary areas (`#F9F9F9`)
- **Disabled/Secondary**: Light gray (`#D1D5DB`)

### Typography
- **Font Family**: Sans-serif (use system fonts or Google Fonts: Inter, Helvetica Neue, or similar)
- **Heading 1 (H1)**: 32px, bold (700), line-height 1.2, letter-spacing -0.02em
- **Heading 2 (H2)**: 24px, bold (700), line-height 1.3, letter-spacing -0.01em
- **Heading 3 (H3)**: 18px, bold (700), line-height 1.4
- **Body**: 16px, regular (400), line-height 1.6
- **Small**: 14px, regular (400), line-height 1.5
- **Caption**: 12px, regular (400), line-height 1.4

### Grid System
- **Base Unit**: 8px
- **Container Max-Width**: 1200px (desktop), 100% (mobile)
- **Columns**: 12-column grid
- **Gutters**: 24px (desktop), 16px (tablet), 8px (mobile)
- **Margins**: Multiples of 8px (8, 16, 24, 32, 40, 48, 56, 64)
- **Padding**: Multiples of 8px (8, 16, 24, 32)

### Visual Elements
- **Dividers**: 1px solid black lines, used sparingly for hierarchy
- **Negative Space**: Generous margins and padding to create breathing room
- **Accent Blocks**: Bold red square elements (8px, 16px, 24px sizes) for emphasis
- **Borders**: Minimal use; prefer white space and dividers
- **Shadows**: None (flat design following Swiss style)
- **Rounded Corners**: None (sharp, geometric edges)

### Layout Principles
- **Asymmetric Composition**: Avoid centered layouts; use offset, grid-based positioning
- **Hierarchy**: Established through size, weight, color (red accents), and white space
- **Alignment**: Strict grid alignment; no arbitrary positioning
- **Density**: Clean, spacious layouts with generous negative space
- **Consistency**: Uniform spacing, typography, and color usage throughout

### Component Styling
- **Buttons**: 
  - Primary: Black text on white background, 1px black border, red accent line on left (4px)
  - Secondary: Black text on white background, 1px black border
  - Hover: Light gray background (`#F9F9F9`)
  - Padding: 12px 16px (body text)

- **Input Fields**:
  - White background, 1px black border
  - Focus: Red border (`#E63946`)
  - Padding: 12px 16px
  - Font: 16px regular

- **Cards/Containers**:
  - White background, 1px black border
  - Padding: 24px
  - No shadows, no rounded corners

- **Progress Indicators**:
  - Red bar on white background
  - Clean, minimal design
  - Percentage text in small font

### Responsive Design
- **Desktop**: Full grid, generous spacing
- **Tablet**: 10-column grid, reduced gutters (16px)
- **Mobile**: Single column, minimal padding (8px)

### Accessibility
- **Contrast**: Black on white meets WCAG AAA standards
- **Focus States**: Red border or background for keyboard navigation
- **Typography**: Minimum 16px for body text on mobile
- **Interactive Elements**: Minimum 44px touch target size

### Do's and Don'ts
- ✓ Use white space generously
- ✓ Maintain strict grid alignment
- ✓ Use red accents sparingly for emphasis
- ✓ Keep typography clean and minimal
- ✓ Use black divider lines for structure
- ✗ Avoid decorative elements beyond red accents
- ✗ Avoid rounded corners or soft shadows
- ✗ Avoid multiple fonts or weights
- ✗ Avoid centered, symmetrical layouts
- ✗ Avoid unnecessary colors or gradients
